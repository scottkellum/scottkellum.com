Scott Kellum

Scott Kellum is the founder of Typetura, a typography as a service company offering both bespoke and ready-made typographic solutions for both web and print. With over 15 years of design experience across type, editorial, digital, and print design; Scott’s experience gives him a unique perspective on the typographic challenges and opportunities in the design industry. He has invented multiple web technologies and projects at Vox Media, Darden Studio, and Roger Black Studio. These accomplishments include inventing new high impact ad formats, parallax techniques, and the fluid typesetting technology that powers Typetura itself.
